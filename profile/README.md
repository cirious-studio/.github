# ⚙️ Cirious Studio

### *Modular infrastructure and systems engineering in Rust 🦀*

---

## About

**Cirious Studio** is an organization focused on research, experimentation, and the development of low-level technologies using **Rust**.

The project explores modern architectures designed around performance, modularity, and predictability, with a particular interest in:

* Infrastructure for game development
* Low-level systems
* Data-oriented architectures
* Reusable and decoupled components
* Experimental high-efficiency tooling

At present, the ecosystem is in an active phase of architectural research and foundational structuring for future open technologies and libraries.

---

# 📐 Engineering Philosophy

The engineering philosophy behind Cirious Studio is guided by principles that prioritize simplicity, efficiency, and long-term technical sustainability.

## ⚡ Data-Oriented Design (DOD)

Exploration of memory layouts and data organization strategies focused on:

* Cache efficiency
* Predictability
* Reduced overhead
* Better utilization of modern hardware

---

## 🚀 Low-Cost Abstractions

Development of APIs and components that seek a balance between:

* Clarity
* Ergonomics
* Explicit control
* Runtime efficiency

---

## 🛡️ Modern Concurrency

Research into parallel models and architectures designed for:

* Multi-core environments
* Memory safety
* Scalability
* Predictable concurrent systems

---

## 🧩 Modularity

Development of independent and decoupled tools that encourage:

* Reusability
* Flexible integration
* Ease of maintenance
* Incremental ecosystem evolution

Our goal is to build an open ecosystem centered around learning, experimentation, and the continuous evolution of high-performance software development.
